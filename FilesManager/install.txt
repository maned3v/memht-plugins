- Compatibility:
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  MemHT 5.0.0.5 Beta and following

========================================================================
- Upload all files in your site root:
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  files/*.* > root/*.*

========================================================================
- Install the plugin:
------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Administration control panel (admin.php) > System > Plugins and pages
  and click on the "Install a new plugin" menu image

  The full url is admin.php?cont=plugins&op=install
